About My Experience

With over 8 years of experience in Blender, I have honed my skills in 3D modeling and sculpting to bring imagination to life. Throughout my journey, I have had the privilege of providing high-quality 3D models for online education, seminars, and instructors, enhancing the learning experience with immersive visuals.

Additionally, I have delved into the exciting realm of virtual reality, where I have crafted intricate and captivating avatars for popular VR experiences. The fusion of technology and artistic expression allows me to create virtual identities that resonate with users, bringing a new level of immersion and engagement.

Beyond the digital realm, I leverage Blender's capabilities to design 3D printable models. By transforming my imagination into tangible objects, I have the opportunity to share my creations with others by selling them or integrating them into my personal projects. The ability to bring physical forms to life through 3D printing is a testament to the versatility of Blender as a powerful tool in my creative arsenal.
